- Between Battles:
  1. [Game Round](../rules/game-round.md) takes place.
  2. [Fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) can enter the outer-rim without having to join the ongoing [Space Battle](space-battle.md).  
  3. [Fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) can enter the inner-side of the [Star System](../pieces/space/star-system.md)(s) where [space](space-battle.md) and/or [Ground Battle](ground-battle.md) take place
      1. If a [Space Battle](space-battle.md) is still ongoing, the new fleet / must join the battle
      2. The [Fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) must respect the general [Space Battle](space-battle.md) rules, including ground reinforcement
  4. Fleets can bring reinforcement that can support the ongoing space / ground battles
  5. [Flagless]  [Fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) can join ongoing battles
  6. [Flagless]  [Fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) can bring ground units to reinforce ground battles.
