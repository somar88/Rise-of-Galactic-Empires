# Damage - Units

[Space](../../pieces/space/fleets.md) and [Ground](../../pieces/ground/ground-forces.md) units are considered damaged if their health is equal or less than half their full health rounded down. e.g.: 3/5 -> not damaged, 2/5 is considered damaged 
- After ending a battle
- After retreating from a battle
A damage Token should be put under the unit(s) to indicate that they have sustained damages and should be repaired.
Damaged units enter a new conflict always with health equal to half of their full health rounded down, even if they have sustained more than that.  [Ideas?]
All damaged buildings with health more than half of their full health rounded down are considered undamaged and have full health.
