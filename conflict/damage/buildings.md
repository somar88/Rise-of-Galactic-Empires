# Damage - Buildings

- [Buildings](../../pieces/ground/ground-structures.md) are considered damaged if their health is equal or less than half their full health rounded down. e.g.: 3/5 -> not damaged, 2/5 is considered damaged 
- After ending a battle
	- A damage Token should be put under the [Building](../../pieces/ground/ground-structures.md)(s) to indicate that they have sustained damages and should be repaired.
- Damaged [Buildings](../../pieces/ground/ground-structures.md) enter a new conflict always with health equal to half of their full health rounded down, even if they have sustained more than that. [Ideas?]
- All damaged [Buildings](../../pieces/ground/ground-structures.md) with health more than half of their full health rounded down are considered undamaged and have full health.
