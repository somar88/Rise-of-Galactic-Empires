- Conflict: 
	- only [Allied Empires](../diplomacy/allied-empires.md) can be located on a [Star System](../pieces/space/star-system.md)
	- only [Allied Armies](../diplomacy/allied-armies.md) can be positioned on a stellar body
	- if non-allied empires & fleets are positioned together on one star system segment: inner-side; outer rim segment. or a planet a [battle](overview.md) is immediately started
	- [Fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) in outer rim segment can be targeted by [planetary defences](../pieces/ground/ground-structures.md) with reach. 
	- [Fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) in inner-side can be targeted by [planetary defences](../pieces/ground/ground-structures.md) with or without reach.
	- [Fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) in [systems](../pieces/space/star-system.md) can be targeted by neighbouring [planetary defences](../pieces/ground/ground-structures.md) with reach
- Separate time lines between [battle](overview.md) and main game round:
	- conflicts are to have more rounds than the game rounds e.g.: 3x conflict rounds, then 1 game round
	- after 3x rounds of [battle/conflict](overview.md) , players should play a [Game Round - done](../rules/game-round.md) as normal excluding the conflict area
	- only traveling around the [Star System](../pieces/space/star-system.md) is allowed and joining conflicts is possible by any other player's [armies](../pieces/ground/ground-forces.md)/[fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md)
	- players should be able to reinforce their [armies](../pieces/ground/ground-forces.md)/[fleets](../pieces/space/fleets.md) between [Game Round - done](../rules/game-round.md)(s) if possible, by entering the [Star System](../pieces/space/star-system.md) for [Space Battle](space-battle.md)(s) and [Landing](landing.md) [armies](../pieces/ground/ground-forces.md) on planets.
