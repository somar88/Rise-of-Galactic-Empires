# Planet Fall

- after player wins a [Ground Battle](ground-battle.md) and if this was an enemy planet a planet fall check should be done
  - D6 roll for each pop 
  - D6: 1-3 -> Remove the pop cube
  - D6: 4-6 -> keep the pop cube and adjust player's resources counter immediately.
