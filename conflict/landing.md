# Landing

- Each empire can try landing armies from the carrying ships that are in the inner-side of the star system or remaining carrying ships after a [Space Battle](space-battle.md) round is done
- For each [planetary defenses](../pieces/ground/ground-structures.md) that is activated, the defending player choose the targeted enemy units and role the dice and assign damage.
