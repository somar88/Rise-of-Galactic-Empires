- #### The Game Round:
	- The Game is played in multiple rounds until one or more [Victory Conditions](victory-conditions.md) are met.
	- A game round consists of two main phases
		1. Player Action & Battle phase
			1. [Player Action](round-phases/action-phase.md):
				- players play cards from hand until all have passed
				- Players who have no more cards at hand must pass
				- Each player starting from the first player plays a card from hand
				- Executes the action
				- Discards the card played to their discard pile
				- Draws to hand size if there is still cards in their empire deck
				- Pass the turn to the next player clockwise
				- After all players have passed or played a card from their hand, initiate a battle phase.
			2. [Conflict Phase](round-phases/conflict-phase.md): consists of this three parts:
				1. [Space Battle](../conflict/space-battle.md): Run space battle for each space conflict
					- start with the conflicts in [players order](player-order.md)
					- multiple battles can take place at the same time if participating players agree
				2. [Landing](../conflict/landing.md) Step
					- Players can initiate invasion / landing(s) or ground reinforcement
				3. [Ground Battle](../conflict/ground-battle.md): run ground battle for each ground conflict
					- start with the conflicts in players order
					- multiple ground battles can take place at the same time if participating players agree
		2. Maintenance phase
			- Each player must pay for empire costs:
				- Fleets not located at a [docking area](docking-area.md)
				- Populations 
				- Any other costs due to active events
		    - Pass first player marker to the next player clockwise
		    - Each player Shuffle the players empire deck and draw to [hand size](hand-size.md)
