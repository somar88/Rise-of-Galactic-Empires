Game Setup:
	- Build the Galaxy:
		- Shuffle [Anomaly Tiles](../world/anomaly-tiles.md) equal to the number of the players into the galactic tiles deck
		- Create the galactic map by placing the Center of the galaxy tile in the middle and then placing drawing a tile from the placing it to make a big hexagonal galaxy
		- Anomaly placement [issues]
	- Each player:
		- takes a set of [Empire Cards](../pieces/cards/empire-cards.md) and shuffle them 
		- Draws 5 Cards from their [Empire Cards](../pieces/cards/empire-cards.md) deck
	    - Decides where home planets located which:
		    - should not be closer to any other player's home planet than the rim systems count divided by the number of players
	    - And then must run an [Exploration](../world/exploration-colonization/exploration.md) turn to populate their star system:
	        1. all planets / moons should be populated
	            - starting star system must have at least one [Industrial-Goods](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguideeconomyresources.md) (IG) district, unless the player is ok with that
	        2. players must adjust their income tableau according to the starting production power of their empire
	    - Takes: 
	        - 4 [Consumer-Goods (CG)](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguideeconomyresources.md)
	        - 4 [Eco](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguideeconomyresources.md)
	        - 2 [Industrial-Goods (IG)](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguideeconomyresources.md)
	    - places a [Scout](../pieces/space/scout.md) unit on one of the outer rim region of their star system
	    - places a [Star-Dock](../pieces/space/star-dock.md) in their home system
	    - places one [Fleet](../pieces/space/fleets.md) token in their home planet
		- places one fleet cards "hidden" with:
	        - one [Flag-Ship](../pieces/space/fleets.md)
	        - one [Destroyer](../pieces/space/fleets.md)
	        - two [Fighters](../pieces/space/fleets.md)
