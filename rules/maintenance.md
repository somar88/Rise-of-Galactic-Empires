# Maintenance

Maintenance is paid during the [Maintenance Phase](round-phases/maintenance-phase.md) at the end of each round cycle. Players must cover all costs — if they cannot, penalties apply.

---

## What Costs Maintenance

### Fleets
| Situation | Cost |
|---|---|
| Fleet located in an owned or War Ally's star system | ✅ Free |
| Fleet located anywhere else | 1× CG + 1× IG per fleet |

### Solo Ships (not part of a fleet)
| Situation | Cost |
|---|---|
| Ship on a friendly planet (owned or War Ally) | ✅ Free |
| Ship anywhere else | 1× IG per ship |

> 🧪 **PLAYTEST:** Test whether solo ship cost should be 1× Eco instead of 1× IG.

### Ground Units
| Situation | Cost |
|---|---|
| Planet with at least one ground unit | 1× CG per planet |

### Ground Buildings
| Situation | Cost |
|---|---|
| Planet with at least one ground building | 1× IG per planet |

### Districts
No maintenance cost. A district's net production already reflects the difference between what it produces and what it consumes.

### Other
- Active event cards may impose additional costs for a set number of rounds as stated on the card.

---

## If You Cannot Pay

If a player's resources are insufficient to cover all maintenance costs:

1. **Trade first** — the player may trade with neighbours or empires they have an active Trade Agreement with to cover the shortfall.
2. **Choose what to skip** — if trading is not enough, the player selects which units, ships, or buildings they will not pay maintenance for.
3. **Draw Chaos Cards** — for each fleet, planet with units, or planet with buildings that goes unpaid, the player draws one [Chaos Card](../pieces/cards/chaos.md) and follows its instructions fully.
