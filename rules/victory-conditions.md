# Victory conditions

Win Conditions: there are several win conditions of the game.
- Moral Win: Control the central galactic planet [Nepiru?] for three sequential game rounds, the player is considered a winner at the end of 3rd game round.
- Political Win: 
- Strategic Win: Control the one third of the galaxy rounded up. Game ends at the end of game round with player controlling one third of the galactic star systems.
