# Player Setup

- Each player:
	- takes a set of [Empire Cards](../pieces/cards/empire-cards.md) and shuffle them 
	- Draws 7 [default hand size](hand-size.md) Cards from their [Empire Cards](../pieces/cards/empire-cards.md) deck
	- Decides where home planets located which:
		- should not be closer to any other player's home planet than the rim systems count divided by the number of players rounded up
	    - And then must run an [Exploration](../world/exploration-colonization/exploration.md) turn to populate their star system:
	        1. all planets / moons should be populated
	            - starting star system must have at least one [Industrial-Goods](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguide03-game-piecescardsresources.md) (IG) district, unless the player is ok with that
	        2. players must adjust their income tableau according to the starting production power of their empire
	- Takes the following resources and add them to the empire storages
		- 4 [Consumer-Goods (CG)](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguide03-game-piecescardsresources.md)
		- 4 [Eco](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguide03-game-piecescardsresources.md)
		- 2 [Industrial-Goods (IG)](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguide03-game-piecescardsresources.md)
	- places a [Scout](../pieces/space/scout.md) unit on one of the outer rim region of their star system
	- places a [Star-Dock](../pieces/space/star-dock.md) in their home system
	- places one [Fleet](../pieces/space/fleets.md) token in their home planet
	- places one fleet cards "hidden" with:
		- one [Flag-Ship](../pieces/space/fleets.md)
		- one [Destroyer](../pieces/space/fleets.md)
		- two [Fighters](../pieces/space/fleets.md)
