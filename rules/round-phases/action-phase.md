# Player Action Phase

- Using Empire [Action Cards](../../pieces/cards/action-cards.md) players play cards from their hand until all have passed
	- Players who have no more cards at hand must pass
	- Each player starting from the first player plays a card from hand
	- Executes the action(s) on the card
	- Discards the card played to their discard pile
	- [Idea](idea.md) Draws to [hand size](../hand-size.md) if there is still cards in their empire deck
	- Pass the turn to the next player clockwise
- After all players have passed or played a card from their hand, initiate next phase.
