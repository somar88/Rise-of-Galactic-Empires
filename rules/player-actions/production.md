# Action Card - Production

Production Action Card:
- Produce resources and goods to the strength of your empire indicated by the resources counters on player tableau
    1. Add the produced resources to the market area
    2. Apply the economic stance transformation for all possible transformation(s)
        - Remove cost directly from market area
        - Add transformed resources directly to the according empire storage area.
    3. Economic stance must be applied!
- Or Change economic stance to a newer one: when chosen, do not produce any resources and therefore no transformation can be done. Basically skip this turn
