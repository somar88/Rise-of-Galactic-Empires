# Empire Cards

Are the combination of all empire starting [Action Cards](action-cards.md) and the [Lore cards](lore-cards.md) that the player has collected through out the game and use as action deck

[Planets](planets.md), [Moons](moons.md) and [Giant Planets](giant-planets.md) cards are not considered empire cards.
