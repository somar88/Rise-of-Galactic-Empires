# Exchange

The exchange [action card|](action-cards.md) allows the player to:
- Initiate a trade agreement, ***or***
- Trade with neighboring empires:
	- Trading can be initiated with any empire which is neighboring the player's empire, ***or*** with other empires that have a [trading agreement](../../TODO.md) with the player, ***or*** with the block market according to the [black market](../../economy/black-market.md) price
