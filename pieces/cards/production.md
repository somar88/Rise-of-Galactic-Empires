# Production

The production [action card|](action-cards.md) allows the player to:
- Produce [resources](../../economy/goods.md) and [goods](../../economy/goods.md) to the strength of the player's empire, which are indicated by the resources counters on [player tableau](../player-tableau.md):
	- Add the produced [01 - Rough Notes/Board-Games/RoGEs/Guide/03 Game Pieces/Cards/Resources](01---rough-notesboard-gamesrogesguide03-game-piecescardsresources.md) to the market area of player's tableau
	- Apply the [economic stance](../../economy/economic-stance.md) for all possible transformation(s) by:
		- removing the cost directly from the market area
		- add transformed resources directly to the empire storage area
- ***or***
- Change the [economic stance](../../economy/economic-stance.md) to a different one and skip production this turn.
