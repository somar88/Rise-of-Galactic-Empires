# Mobilization

The mobilization action card allows the player to:
- Move a fleet or a civilian ship to their [Movement Strength](../../rules/movement.md), ***or***
- move up to 3 flagless fleets from a neighboring to a system to reinforce a space battle or transfer ground unites to an ongoing land battle
- Movement may be interrupted if:
	- [Scout](../space/scout.md): if entered a unexplored [Star System](../space/star-system.md) and then perform [exploration](../../world/exploration-colonization/exploration.md)
	- All other [ships](ships.md): when encountering a none allied pieces(s) and initiate [space battle](../../conflict/space-battle.md)
	- Fleets: after performing a [landing](../../conflict/landing.md)
	- Colony Ship: after preforming colonization
