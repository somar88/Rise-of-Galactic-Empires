# Action Cards

Each player begins the game with empire starting deck, which consist of twelve cards :
- 4x [Mobilization](mobilization.md) action cards
- 3x [Production](production.md) action cards
- 4x [Civilian](civilian.md) action cards
- 1x [Exchange](exchange.md) action card
and additional [Lore cards](lore-cards.md) depending on the empire picked by the player.
