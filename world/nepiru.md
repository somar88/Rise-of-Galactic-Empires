# Nepiru — The Galactic Core

Nepiru is the ancient central planet of the galaxy, present on the board from the very start of the game. It was once home to a civilization of unimaginable advancement, now long silent. Their automated systems still hum. Something may still be listening.

Holding Nepiru is one of the most powerful strategic positions in the game — and the key to a **Moral Victory**.

---

## Golden Districts

Nepiru has **5 Golden Districts**. Golden Districts are self-sustaining and produce at **2× the normal output** of a standard district.

- Golden Districts do not require population tokens to produce — they are fully automated.
- They still count toward maintenance as normal.

---

## Moral Victory

A player who holds Nepiru for **3 consecutive Maintenance Phases** wins the game by Moral Victory.

The Nepiru Story Arc is tied directly to this — completing all 3 arc steps coincides with achieving the Moral Win.

---

## The Nepiru Story Arc

The Story Arc activates the moment a player first takes control of Nepiru. It represents the holder's relationship with the ancient legacy of the planet.

### Opening

When Nepiru is first controlled, the holding player reveals the Nepiru Story Arc card, places a progress token on **Step 0**, and immediately chooses one of three paths.

### Choosing a Path

| Path | Theme |
|---|---|
| 🔍 The Excavation | Archaeological discovery — uncover what was left behind |
| ⚙️ The Reactivation | Restore the ancient systems — bring the machine back online |
| 👁️ The Communion | Make contact with whatever survived |

The player advances one step along their chosen path at the end of each Maintenance Phase they hold Nepiru.

---

## Path: 🔍 The Excavation

| Step | Name | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | First Dig | Draw 2 Event cards, keep 1 |
| 2 | The Archive | Permanent +1 to one production type of your choice |
| 3 | The Vault | Gain a unique Artefact card with a permanent passive ability |

---

## Path: ⚙️ The Reactivation

| Step | Name | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Power On | Nepiru district maintenance is free for 2 rounds |
| 2 | Systems Online | Nepiru gains a free Defense Artillery System |
| 3 | Full Activation | Gain 3 mechanized units of any mix at no cost (Armored Vehicle, Advanced Armored Vehicle, Mobile Artillery — no Space Marines) |

---

## Path: 👁️ The Communion

| Step | Name | Reward |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | First Signal | Peek at 3 cards from any deck |
| 2 | Dialogue | Propose one binding ceasefire that cannot be refused |
| 3 | Convergence | Gain a unique empire passive ability |

---

## When Nepiru Is Conquered

If Nepiru changes hands mid-arc:

- The new owner **inherits the current path and step** — they continue where the previous holder left off.
- The new owner may choose to **reset to the branch point** (back to choosing a path), but this costs 1 step of progress — the arc token moves back by 1.
- The Moral Victory counter (consecutive Maintenance Phases) resets to 0 for the new owner.
