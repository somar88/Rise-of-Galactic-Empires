# Rise of Galactic Empires

## General:
- RoGEs: is an 5eX game which takes place in space. 5eX game means:
	1. [Explore](../world/exploration-colonization/exploration.md): explore new star systems, find new habitable world and other empires
	2. [Expand](../world/exploration-colonization/colonization.md): colonize new planets, star systems 
	3. [Exploit](../rules/player-actions/production.md): extract resources available on the new worlds
	4. [Exchange](../rules/player-actions/exchange.md): trade with other players and NP empires
	5. [Exterminate](../conflict/overview.md): start wars against other players, try to conquer them and make friends of them

## World:
-  In a galaxy taming with life, these spacefaring alien civilizations are competing to take control of the resources of this galaxy, facing lots of challenges
