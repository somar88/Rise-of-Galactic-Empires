# Resources

- Resources:
	- **Consumer goods**: 
		- represented by colorful cubes, CGs are yours to pay for maintenance of population a player have in their empire. Or pay for production of more pops
	- **Economic Power (Energy)**: 
		- represented by currency like tokens, are used for any kind of payment like buying goods and trading.
	- **Industrial Good:** 
		- represented by small gear like tokens, are used for building space ships advance buildings on stellar bodies, in general are used for constructing/producing non-pops pieces
- These recourses can be produced by production areas called districts located onboard of the stellar bodies:
	- each district may be able to produced one or multiple production goods
	- in general there are these types of production districts: basic districts which are Consumer Goods, Economic, Industrial, Dual-Production (only Consumer Goods or Economic, Omni-Production all production goods)
	- what type of production goods a district is producing is determined by the player when assigning populations on them
	- switching the type of production in Dual | Omni Production districts will mean the district will not generate this round any goods and go into transition phase
	- to take control of a district, the player should put/deploy a population cube (on inactive face) on the district indicating it is populated but not producing yet
	- when the population cube occupying the district is turn on the active face, the count number of corresponding district production type should be increased by one on the player's economy tableau immediately and vice versa
	- industrial districts can be populated only if all of other districts are fully occupied
	- in the production phase, the players collect resources and gold tokens as indicated on the player's economy tableau
